Materi Matematika

BARISAN DAN DERET

A. BARISAN ARITMATIKA

Rumus Suku ke-n

\[ U_n = a + (n-1)b \]

Contoh

Barisan: 2, 5, 8, 11, ...
\[ a = 2,\quad b = 3 \] \[ U_n = 2 + (n-1)3 \]
---

B. DERET ARITMATIKA

Rumus Jumlah n Suku

\[ S_n = \frac{n}{2}(2a + (n-1)b) \]
\[ S_5 = \frac{5}{2}(2(2) + 4(3)) = 40 \]
---

C. BARISAN GEOMETRI

Rumus Suku ke-n

\[ U_n = a \cdot r^{n-1} \]
\[ a = 2,\ r = 3 \] \[ U_n = 2 \cdot 3^{n-1} \]
---

D. DERET GEOMETRI

Rumus Jumlah n Suku

\[ S_n = \frac{a(r^n - 1)}{r - 1} \]
---

Deret Tak Hingga

\[ S_\infty = \frac{a}{1 - r}, \quad |r| < 1 \]
---
